### **The Problem**
- **$2.84 trillion** lost annually due to software downtime and errors
- Developers spend **40-50%** of their time debugging
- **73%** of companies report increasing error complexity

- AI-powered error clustering (50% faster resolution)
- Real-time monitoring (<10ms latency)

- 5-minute setup vs 2-3 days for competitors
